>*Never seen without their mask. Poorly understood, but begrudgingly appreciated due to their natural talents.*

Vesperians are described as a beetle themed [[Races|race]] with organically grown masks and very dark skin. They are most commonly seen at Greathive Aratel and are mostly under ranked by fellow Ganymedes. Vesperians are more durable than others and have a superior mental stability. They also have a natural exoskeleton that regenerates when you rest.

**Bonuses:**
"_You have a layer of natural armor that replenishes when you rest._"
* Chitin:
	* You gain +10 AC against all physical damage types.
* +2 Fortitude
* +2 Willpower
