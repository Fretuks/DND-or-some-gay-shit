> _Deer-horned pariahs who lived a peaceful life as nomads before the tides came. Tolerant of extremes that many others would avoid.

Gremors are a [[Races|race]] similar to deer. They appear with greenish, blue or brown skin, with horns. They are stronger and more durable than most. Additionally, they get Advantage on Survival Throws and a lower starvation rate, as well as boosted reputation with The Children Of Navae.
