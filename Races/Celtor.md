> *Silver-tongued traders whose pride often gets the better of them. Renowned as ship-builders and seafarers alike.*

Celtors are a common [[Races|race]] with a pale-ish green skin, grey hair and a racoon-like marking on their face. Celtor are based on the real life Mustelidae (e.g. Racoons and Racoon-likes) family. Celtors are known as intelligent and charismatic, and renowned as seafarers. Celtors are portrayed as rich, greedy, and prideful. They formerly had a place to call home, the Celtorian City, but was sunken into The Depths.
