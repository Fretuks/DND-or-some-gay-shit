>*Deeply spiritual folk who have an uncanny knack for deciphering the emotions of others while keeping their own a closed book.

The Chrysid people are a [[Races|race]] related to moths, inheriting their compound eyes and feathery antennae. They are a rare race to see and carry close ties with the other races of Greathive despite their conflicting view on technology. They are faster than most other races and are surprisingly charismatic. They are also excellent at tracking their prey.

**Bonuses:**
