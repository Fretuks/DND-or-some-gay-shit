> *With canine features, the Canor are fiercely loyal. Often found as guards and personal servants in the retinue of powerful people.*

Canor are an uncommon [[Races|race]] considered to have the characteristics of a canine but the attributes and features of a lion. Canor appear with a mane and varying facial markings around their eyes. The Canor are stronger and more charismatic than most races, and they are loyal to their allies, dealing reduced damage to and from guildmates. The race of Canor is also heavily favored by the Authority, though not nearly as much as the Adret. Canor have three distinct facial markings.

**Bonuses:**
“Your bond with your allies is strong enough to reduce damage between you.”_
* Loyalty:  
	* Allies take 50% less damage from you and deal 50% less damage to you.
	* Allies within 6 meters gain +5 AC.
	* When an ally within 6 meters is knocked, you gain Advantage on your next attack.
* +2 Strength
* +2 Charisma
