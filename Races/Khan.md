> *Brimming with pride yet attentive to the finer details. Known as steadfast warriors and mercenaries the world over.*

Khans are a rare [[Races|race]] with light skin, and a monkey-like appearance. Khans have a special crown on their forehead which they and they alone possess. Khans are stronger and faster than most, and they also have a great understanding of weaponry. This makes them good allies with The Hundred Legions.

**Bonuses:**
"You learn how to use new pieces of equipment quicker than others.”
* Versatile:
	* You have proficiency in most weapon types and can use them in any combination.
	* You are unable to use guns as they are 'dishonorable'.
* +2 Strength
* +2 Agility
