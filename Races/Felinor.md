>*Akin to their feline features, the Felinor are carefree and whimsical. Seen to be thieves and outlaws, but merely seek a life of freedom.*

One of the most distinct races in the Lumen for their infamous cat ears, Felinors garnered the most attention of all races in Lumen. They are a rare [[Races|race]] with little social or political influence to their race, mainly varying with the individual themselves. Felinors are charismatic and agile and have improved stealth.

**Bonuses:**
"Relying on your instincts, you are naturally more stealthy. You are more nimble on wooden surfaces._"
* Nightchild:  
	* Your climb speed is 1.5x your walk speed, when climbing wooden surfaces its 2x instead.
	* You have Advantage on Stealth Throws
	* Once per encounter, automatically succeed a failed Dodge check
* +2 Dexterity
* +2 Charisma
