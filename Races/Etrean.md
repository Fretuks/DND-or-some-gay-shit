>*Defined by their snake-like features and cunning. Isolationists who tend to their own interests.*

Etreans are described as a snake-like [[Races|race]], who commonly have pale skin, and slit eyes. They are the most common race and can be found most commonly at Etris. Etreans are known for their Intelligence and agility, as well as resistance to status effects. Etreans are commonly depicted as racists.
