> *Solitary folk seldom seen below the clouds. Sharp and independent, though strongly protective of those who earn their respect.*

Tirans are an extremely rare bird-like [[Races|race]]. They have special circle-like markings on their face. They have are known for their superior agility and mental clarity and take less fall damage.

**Bonuses:**
"As a show of independance when they come of age, Tirans will strike out on their own with just their hand-crafted glider, gliding down from the mountains peaks where they make their homes.”
* Feathered Glider
	* You have resistance to fall damage
	* You start with an unbreakable feathered glider. The descent rate of it is also slightly lower than regular gliders, making you able to travel slightly further.
* Gain Advantage on initiative rolls
* +2 Willpower
* +2 Agility
