A creature afflicted with Burning is engulfed in persistent elemental flames.
**While Burning:**
- The creature takes **1d4 fire damage** at the start of each of its turns.
- Burning lasts for **3 rounds** unless removed early.
- Reapplying Burning **refreshes the duration**, but does not stack damage.
- Burning may be removed early by:
    - Entering water
    - Being affected by [[Chill]]
    - Using an action to extinguish the flames
    - Dodging
