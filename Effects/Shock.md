Shock disrupts the nervous system with violent electrical discharge.
**When Shock is applied:**
- The creature is **stunned until the end of the current turn** (≈ 1 second).
   - This stun **interrupts hyperarmor and ongoing actions**, but:
    * Does not prevent Parrying
    - Does not prevent Reactions
- Passive posture regeneration is disabled until the end of the creature’s next turn.

**While Shocked:**
- Movement speed is reduced by **25%** until the end of the creature’s next turn.
- Shock does **not** stack; reapplication refreshes duration.
