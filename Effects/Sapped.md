Your ether flow is forcibly destabilized, leaving your reserves sluggish and primed for violent discharge.
**While Sapped:**
- Your **Mantra cooldowns are increased by 50%**.
- You may still cast [[Combat/Mantras]].
- Sapped lasts for **2 rounds**, unless otherwise specified.
- When Sapped **ends**, the creature is immediately [[Shock|Shocked]].
- The Shock applied when Sapped ends:
    - Follows all normal **Shock** rules.
    - Occurs even if the creature is immune to lightning damage.
- Sapped does **not stack**; reapplication refreshes duration.
