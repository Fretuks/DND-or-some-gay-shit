While Staggered:
- You cannot Parry or Dodge.
- Attacks against you automatically **crit**.
- You regain **all Posture** at the end of the staggered round.

While a creature is Staggered, **anyone** may use an action to perform a **Critical**.
A Critical:
 - Deals normal scaled damage
 - Adds the weapon’s **Critical Modifier**
 - Ignores blocking and parrying
