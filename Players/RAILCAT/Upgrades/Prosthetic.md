# Branch 1 - Offensive Modules

---
## Impact Governor
When Torque is consumed:
- Also deal +2 Posture damage.
If the target is Sapped:
- Deal an additional +1 Posture.

---
## Voltage Injector
When you Parry a Sapped enemy:
- Immediately deal 1d6 Lightning (separate instance).
If this causes Shock:
- Target loses 1 additional Posture.

---
##  Duelist’s Executioner
If you strike a Shocked target with an unarmed attack:
- Add +1d8 Blunt.
- Gain 5 penetration for that attack
Once per turn.

---
## Railbreaker Knuckles
When you Riposte:
- Add +1d6 Blunt.
- If target is Burning → deal +1d6 Fire.

---
# Branch 2 - Defensive Modules

---
## Counterbalance Gyro
When you fail a Parry:
- Reduce incoming Posture damage by 2.
Once per round.

---
## Shock Dampener Lattice
When Shocked:
- Movement reduction is halved.
- Passive posture regen is not disabled.

---
## Reactive Plating
When reduced below 50% Posture:
- Gain +5 Physical AC until start of next turn
Once per 3 rounds.

---
## Feral Stabilizer (Capstone Defensive)
While in Feral State:
- You regain 1 Posture per turn.
- Parry failures do not cause additional posture penalties.

---
# Branch 3 — Utility Modules

---
## Grapple Line Emitter
Bonus Action (CD 5):
- Pull a target 3 meters toward you.
- If they collide with terrain:
    - Deal 1d6 Blunt.
Does not apply Sapped.

---
## Vector Thrusters
After a successful Parry:
- You may move 3 meters without provoking reactions.

---
## Overclocked Recall
Magnetic Recall now:
- Grants +3 meters movement speed until end of turn.
- Your next attack gains +5 Penetration

---
## Kinetic Lock
If you Parry the same target twice in 2 rounds:
- Their next Dodge is made at disadvantage.
