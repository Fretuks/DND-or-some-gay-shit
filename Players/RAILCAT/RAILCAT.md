'Railcat' is what Summer Company officers call her in reports when they do not wish to write her real name.

Among the Felinors, freedom is instinct. They climb where they wish. They steal what shines. They laugh at danger and slip through fingers like smoke. A Felinor belongs to no one. She once embodied that perfectly.

A former Deepdelver of the Ignition Union, she descended into the Depths with a grin and came back with monsters carved into memory.

Then she lost her arm, her blade, her choice. Now she serves the Summer Company. Not as a patriot, but as an asset. She helps them because she must. And that is the cruelest cage of all for a Felinor. She still smiles. She still tilts her head playfully. She still makes jokes before a fight.

But when the duel begins, the air changes, her tail stops moving... And someone is about to lose.

- She is not Staggered.
	- She still takes the full HP damage of the breaking attack.
