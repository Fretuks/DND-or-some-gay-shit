Axiom-07 does not carry traditional weapons. It integrates **Hive-Pattern Tool Modules** into its forearm mounts.

After each long rest, you may equip:
- 1 Primary Module
- 1 Secondary Module 
- 1 Special Module

## WM-01 – Ether Pulse Emitter ("Generic Gun")
A short-range Ether calibration emitter. Originally designed to realign unstable constructs. Emits compressed force pulses.
**Module**: Primary
**Damage:** 1d6 Force  
**Posture Damage:** 2  
**Penetration:** 5%  
**Chip Damage:** 5%  
**Riposte Modifier:** +1d6 Force  
**Range:** 12 meters/36 meters  
**Cost:** 1 Ether per shot  
**Properties:** Light
### Ether Disruption Protocol
If a target is hit twice in the same round:
- Target loses 1 Ether at the start of their next turn.

## WM-02 – Impact Driver ("Metal Pipe")
A compact kinetic actuator used to seat Hive plating and drive structural anchors into place. Repurposed as a blunt combat tool.
**Module**: Secondary
**Damage:** 1d6 Blunt  
**Posture Damage:** 4  
**Penetration:** 5%  
**Chip Damage:** 0%  
**Riposte Modifier:** +1d6 Blunt  
**Properties:** Medium
### Structural Pressure Protocol
When hitting a target that is **blocking**:
- +1 additional Posture damage.

# WM-S01 – Ether Stabilizer Node
A palm-mounted Ether tuning fork used to recalibrate unstable constructs and prevent Ether cascade failure.
**Module:** Special  
**Action:** Bonus Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 rounds  
### Stabilization Pulse
Target creature within 6 meters:
Choose one:
- Restore 2 Ether
- Restore 2 Posture
- Remove one minor status effect (Burning, Chilled, Slowed)
Cannot target self two rounds in a row.
