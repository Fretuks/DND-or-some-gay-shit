Axiom-07 was one of the earliest Hive construct prototypes. It is slim, lightly armored, built around a 7cm artificial Life Orb and a visible Ether containment core. Unlike later Hive constructs, the orb did not merely animate the chassis.

The Chrysid engineer and the bound Ganymede responsible for the project made a decision the Hive did not approve of: They released it.

Now Axiom-07 travels Lumen freely, curious, cheerful, dangerous and independent, while Hive retrieval units hunt it down.
