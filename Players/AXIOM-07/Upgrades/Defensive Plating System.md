# DP-01 – Physical Reinforcement Lattice
---
## DP-01-A – Densified Alloy Weave
**Cost:** 2 Scout Parts
Additional micro-layer plating installed beneath primary ribbing.
**Effect:**
- +5 Blunt AC
- +5 Slash AC
- +5 Pierce AC
---
## DP-01-B – Reactive Impact Bracing
**Cost:** 3 Seeker Parts
Internal supports redistribute force on heavy impacts.
**Effect:**
- When you take 15+ damage from a single physical hit:
    - Reduce that damage by 5 after mitigation.
- Does not apply to elemental damage.
---
## DP-01-C – Adaptive Armor Shifting
**Cost:** 4 Hunter Parts
Armor plates subtly reorient when detecting repeated attack vectors.
**Effect:**
- When hit twice in a round by the same physical damage type:
    - Gain +5 AC against that type until the start of your next turn.
---
# DP-02 – Elemental Conductivity Profile
---
## DP-02-A – Thermal Shielding Mesh
**Cost:** 2 Scout Parts
Heat-resistant mesh layered around Ether conduits.
**Effect:**
- Fire AC improves from -25 → -15.
- The first Fire hit each combat deals 25% less damage (after mitigation).
---
## DP-02-B – Lightning Grounding Rod
**Cost:** 3 Seeker Parts
Grounding prongs installed along lower chassis.
**Effect:**
- Lightning AC improves from -25 → -15.
- The first Lightning hit each combat deals 25% less damage (after mitigation).
---
## DP-02-C – Stabilized Ether Routing
**Cost:** 4 Hunter Parts
Ether flow is rerouted through insulated channels to prevent overload.
**Effect:**
- Fire AC improves from -15 → -5.
- Lightning AC improves from -15 → -5.
---
## DP-02-D – Entropic Dampener Field
**Cost:** 5 Hunter Parts
A thin fluctuating field disperses high-energy impact before contact.
**Effect:**
- Once per round, when you take Elemental damage:
    - Reduce it by 5 after mitigation.
- Does stack with Reactive Impact Bracing.
