**Posture:** 14
Regain (**Posture ÷ 10, rounded down**) Posture at the start of your turn.
-> Lightweight exoskeletal structure. Minimal plating. 
## CC-02 - Ether Containment Battery
**Ether Capacity:** 30
Regain **2 Ether** at the start of your next turn if:
- You dealt damage last round OR
- You moved at least 6m OR
- You were targeted by an attack.
-> Visible containment chamber adjacent to Life Orb housing.
