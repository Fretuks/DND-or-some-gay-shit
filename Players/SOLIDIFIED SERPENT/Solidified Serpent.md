A field operative used when the Summer Company needs fortifications removed, ambushes erased, or clustered enemies dispersed quickly. Unlike most Company agents who rely on revolvers and precision rifles, this operative carries a compact recoilless launcher - the **Non-Magic-Missile (NMM)**.

The weapon is not designed for dueling. It is designed to delete groups. The Enforcer operates quietly until the moment of engagement. One rocket. One blast. Then reposition before retaliation arrives.

They are Etrean-born and carry the cultural arrogance typical of their homeland, openly dismissive of foreigners and particularly critical of the other party members.

# Signature Traits
### Summer Company Demolition Training
Explosive weapons are notoriously unwieldy. The Enforcer has been trained to deploy them efficiently.
- Firing the **NMM** does **not provoke opportunity attacks**.
- After firing the NMM, you may **move up to 3 meters without triggering reactions**.
### Tactical Reload Doctrine
The launcher normally requires **1 action to reload**.
However:
- An ally within **1.5 meters** may spend a **Bonus Action** to assist the reload.
### Calculated Detonation
The Enforcer understands blast radii.
When firing the NMM:
- Allies within the blast radius take **half damage**.
- Enemies caught in the **outer half of the blast radius** take **+1 posture damage** from concussive force.
### Etrean Supremacy
Raised under Etrean doctrine of cultural superiority, the Enforcer has little patience for outsiders.
- **+2 on Intimidation checks** against non-Etreans.
- **-2 on Persuasion checks** when dealing with non-Etrean allies or factions.
