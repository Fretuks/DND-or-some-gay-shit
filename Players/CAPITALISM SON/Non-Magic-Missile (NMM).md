Compact recoilless rocket launcher used by Summer Company demolition teams.

Length: **~55 cm**. Carried on the back.
### Weapon Profile
**Damage:** 2d8 Fire + 1d8 Force  
**Posture Damage:** 4 (NB)  
**Penetration:** 20%  
**Chip Damage:** 40%  
**Riposte Modifier:** -
**Blast Radius:** 4.5 meters  
Targets within radius are hit individually.
**Properties:** Heavy, Two-Handed, Explosive, Loud, Unparryable

### Reload
After firing:
- The launcher must be **reloaded for 1 round** before firing again.
- Reloading normally consumes the **entire turn**.

The Enforcer begins encounters with 3 Rockets. More are gained from summer company outposts or during short rests.
