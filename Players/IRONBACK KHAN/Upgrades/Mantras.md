### 1-Star Mantras
Available from **Level 1**
### 2-Star Mantras
Begin appearing at **Level 5**
### 3-Star Mantras
Begin appearing at **Level 9**

---
# 1-STAR MANTRAS
---
## Iron Crash
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 4 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 2 Rounds
Leap 3 meters and slam.
- 1d10 Blunt damage (3m radius)
- +3 Posture damage
- Structures take double damage.

---
## Hammerfist
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 3 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 1 Round
Single-target crushing strike.
- 1d12 Blunt damage
- +2 Posture damage
- If target is Prone, add +1d6 damage.

---
## Body Check
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 3 Ether  
**Action:** Bonus Action  
**Cooldown:** 2 Rounds
Move 3 meters into a creature.
- 1d8 Blunt damage
- 2 Posture damage
- Target must pass Strength save or be pushed 3 meters.

---
## Groundbreaker
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 4 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Punch ground in a 3m line.
- 1d8 Blunt damage
- Creatures must save or fall Prone.

---
## Iron Advance
**Type:** Mobility (Offensive)  
**Cost:** 3 Ether  
**Action:** Bonus Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Move 4 meters straight.
- First creature struck takes 1d6 Blunt and 2 Posture

---
# 2-STAR MANTRAS
---
## Break the Line
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 6 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Sweeping horizontal strike.
- 2d8 Blunt damage in 4.5m cone
- +5 Posture damage    
- Creatures blocking take +3 posture damage.

---
## Legion Uppercut
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 5 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Devastating vertical strike.
- 2d10 Blunt damage
- Target is launched 3m upward
- Takes 3 additional Posture damage
- Falls Prone on landing.

---
## Iron Lock
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 5 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Make grapple attempt.
On success:
- Target is grappled.
- Deal 1d10 Blunt damage at the start of each turn.
- Target has disadvantage on checks to escape.
Grapple ends if you are staggered.

You may only maintain one Iron Lock at a time.

---
## Pulverize
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 7 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 4 Rounds
Two-hit combo.
- 1st hit: 1d10 Blunt
- 2nd hit: 2d8 Blunt
- Total +4 Posture damage

---
## Unbreakable Charge
**Type:** Mobility (Offensive)  
**Cost:** 6 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 4 Rounds
Move up to 6 meters in straight line.
All creatures in path:
- 1d10 Blunt damage
- 4 Posture damage
- Cannot be interrupted by reactions.

---
## Shockwave Step
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 5 Ether  
**Action:** Bonus Action  
**Cooldown:** 3 Rounds
Stomp ground.
- All adjacent enemies take 1d6 Blunt
- Lose 3 Posture

---
# 3-STAR MANTRAS
---
## Cataclysm Drop
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 10 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 5 Rounds
Leap up to 6 meters and slam.
- 3d10 Blunt damage (3m radius)
- 8 Posture damage
- Structures automatically crack or shatter.

---
## Legion Execution
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 9 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 4 Rounds
Single-target annihilation strike.
- 4d8 Blunt damage
- +10 Posture damage
- If target is Staggered, add +2d8 damage.

---
## Living Catapult
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 8 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 5 Rounds
Grab creature and throw 9 meters.
- 3d10 Blunt to target
- 1d10 splash
- Target loses 6 Posture
- Falls Prone.

---
## Worldbreaker Combo
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 12 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** 6 Rounds
Three-hit sequence:
1. 2d8 Blunt
2. 2d8 Blunt
3. 3d10 Blunt
Total +8 Posture damage across sequence.
If final hit reduces posture to 0, target is knocked 4.5 meters and Prone.

---
## Indomitable Rampage
**Type:** Support 
**Cost:** 11 Ether  
**Action:** Action  
**Cooldown:** Once per Combat
For 3 rounds:
- All unarmed attacks deal +1d8 damage
* +3 Posture damage on the first hit each turn
- You cannot be Slowed, Stunned, Grappled or Paralyzed while above 1 Posture.

---
## Fortress Impact
**Type:** Combat  
**Cost:** 10 Ether  
**Action:** Reaction (when hit)  
**Cooldown:** 4 Rounds
After taking damage:
- Immediately deal 2d10 Blunt to attacker
- Attacker loses posture equal to your Constitution Modifier + 4.
