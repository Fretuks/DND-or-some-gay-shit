The Skaven grew up in **Iron Meridian**, a massive industrial city controlled by the Central Authority. The city is built around factories, smelters, and mining operations. Most Skaven there survive by stealing food, selling information, or doing dangerous work no one else wants.

He spent most of his early life in the lower districts, living off scraps and theft. Skaven communities in the city are unstable. Groups form and break apart quickly, and fights over territory are common. Learning to run, hide, and stab faster than the other rat was usually enough to survive.

Eventually he was caught stealing from a warehouse owned by the Authority. Instead of executing him, they sent him to the mines outside the city. Cheap labor was more useful. The mines produced a strange ore. Workers called it **Black Meridian Ore**. The Authority banned its consumption because miners kept getting sick or unstable after handling it. The Skaven discovered something different.

If the ore was ground into powder and mixed with other substances, for example alcohol, fungus cultures, certain chemicals used in minings, it produced a stimulant. The raw ore itself did nothing except create strong cravings, but mixtures created powerful temporary effects.

Miners sometimes traded these mixtures secretly. The Skaven learned how to refine them better than most. That is where his addiction started. Over time he learned to produce multiple **variants** of the drug by mixing the ore with different reagents.

The Skaven was considered too problematic to keep in the mines. He was caught with refined ore mixtures and blamed for distributing them. Instead of executing him, the overseers sold him to a merchant broker. Skaven are sometimes used as cheap meat cargo in long-distance shipments. Smaller races are easy to transport and not considered valuable enough to protect.

- Count as **Small** for grapple, pull, drag and shove interactions.
### Addict
* Your body has adapted to Black Meridian stimulants.
* While under the effect of a Black Meridian drug:
	- Gain **+1 Posture Regain**
	- Gain **+1.5 meter Movement Speed**
- Withdrawal begins after **24 hours** without a dose.

### Withdrawal
If the Skaven goes **24 hours without consuming a dose**, withdrawal begins.
- Movement Speed **-3 meters**
- Posture Regain **-2**
- Ether Regain **-2**
- Lose **1 sanity** every hour.
