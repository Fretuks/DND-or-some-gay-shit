Gravity Field is a utility-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on soft crowd control.

When activated, it creates a 9m diameter sphere for 1d4 + 1 rounds.  
Enemies inside:
- Cannot dodge, jump, or use mobility [[Combat/Mantras]]
Allies inside:
- Gain +10 AC
- Gain +2 on attack rolls

When corrupted, enemies entering the field are pulled 3m toward the center and cannot parry while inside.
