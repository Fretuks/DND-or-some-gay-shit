Sacred Field is a support-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on debuffing enemies.

When activated, you create a circular field with a 9 meter radius centered on you. Enemies within this field suffer -3 to attack rolls and -5 to damage rolls.

When corrupted, Enemies cannot critically hit while inside the field and take 1d10 radiant damage on missed attacks. They also lose 1 Posture at the start of each of their turns.
