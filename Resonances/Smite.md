Smite is an offense-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on passive damage over time.

You need to spend a **bonus action** to active this resonance. When activated, you summon 6 orbs above you. Each round, one orb will crash into a random enemy within 9 meters, dealing 1d10 necrotic damage.

Shards:
- **Ignore Dodge reactions**
- **Deal double posture damage**

When corrupted, you gain 8 orbs instead of 6 and each round, two orbs will be fired. The last 2 orbs will deal triple damage.
