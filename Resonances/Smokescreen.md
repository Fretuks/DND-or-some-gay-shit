Smokescreen is a utility-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on soft crowd control.

When activated, you create a thick cloud of black smoke with a 4.5 meter radius, centered on your position. The cloud lasts for 5 rounds. If enemies step inside the cloud, they will be blinded until they leave the cloud. You will be invisible while in the cloud.

When corrupted, enemies inside will be silenced and take 1d6 poison damage per round. Enemies leaving the smoke will be disoriented for one turn.
