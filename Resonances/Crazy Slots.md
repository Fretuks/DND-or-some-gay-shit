Crazy Slots is a utility-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on ~~gambling~~ giving you a unique weapon for each encounter.

When activated, You gain a random weapon out of a pool. Throw a 1d6: 
- 1: Divine Axe 
- 2: Divine Greatsword 
- 3: Divine Spear 
- 4: Divine Longsword 
- 5: Divine Dagger 
- 6: Divine Flintlock
Weapon vanishes early if dropped.

When Corrupted, you roll two weapons and choose one.  When the weapon expires, it detonates, dealing 2d10 radiant/necrotic (your choice) in a 3m radius.
