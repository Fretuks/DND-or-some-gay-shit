Shard Bow is an offense-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on quick DPS from a distance.

You need to spend a **bonus action** to active this resonance. When activated, you summon a radiant bow with 3 radiant shards that can be fired individually.
- Range: **35m**
- Damage: **1d12 radiant / Shard**

Shards:
- **Ignore Dodge reactions**
- **Deal double posture damage**

When corrupted, you gain 5 shards instead of 3. You can fire all shards at once in a devastating **Shardfall Barrage**:
- All remaining shards rain down in a **6-meter radius**
- Each shard deals **full damage** to a random enemy in the area
- Each enemy can only be hit by **2 shards max**
