Windup is an offense-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on heavy burst DPS.

Takes one action to activate. When activated, your next attack is a guaranteed Supercritical, dealing 2.5x the normal damage and stunning the target for 1 round. Can be stacked with a normal critical attack to be a Hypercritical Attack, dealing 3x damage instead.

When corrupted, if you kill an enemy using this ability, its cooldown will be refunded.
