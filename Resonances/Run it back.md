Run it back is a utility-based [[Resonances|Resonance]], focused on repositioning.

When activated, you place a mark on the ground. After 1 round, you teleport back to it, healing all the damage you took during the turn. If you take more than 25% of your Max HP as damage, your teleport will be cancelled.

When corrupted, you can choose to activate the recall at any point, as long as you're within 18 meters of the mark.
