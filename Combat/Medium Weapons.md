**Examples:** Swords, Spears, Clubs, Rifles, Twinblades
Medium weapons are the **baseline** and most versatile category, balancing offense, defense, and pressure.

### Benefits
- **No inherent penalties**
- **Balanced Weapon Weight**
    - Reliable Posture damage
- **Moderate Penetration**
    - Typically 5–10 Penetration
- **Flexible Mantra Compatibility**
    - Most mantras are balanced around Medium weapons

### Drawbacks
- **No specialization bonuses**
- **Limited Chip Damage**
    - Usually 0–5%
- **Average Mobility**
    - No dodge or movement bonuses
