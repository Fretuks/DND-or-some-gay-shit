Arcwarder is an [[Oaths|Oath]] focusing heavily on defense. Four of its passives are defensive, and the other two are mobility. 

>"You vow to be a shield for your comrades, to wear your regalia with pride and serve the greater collective. From each according to their ability, to each according to their needs."

The Arc Suit is a defence orientated piece of semi-sentient armour that [[The Ignition Union]] bestows upon their most trusted. It fuses with the soul of those who swear the oath, allowing them to manifest it around them at will. Arcwarders are sworn to become a conduit of protection to their fellow Union mates and do whatever it takes to defend them. Notably, Arcwarders are the only people known to utilize Plasma in their attacks, likely from a result of the Flamecharm and Thundercall being combined within the suit.

Arc Suit: You manifest your semi-sentient Arc Suit around your body.
- **Type:** Combat Mantra  
- **Activation:** Bonus Action 
- **Duration:** 4 rounds  
- **Cooldown:** 10 rounds  
- **Ether Cost:** 4
- While Active:
	- Gain **+10 Physical AC** (Blunt, Slash, Pierce only)
	- Gain **+10 Fire & Lightning AC
	- Gain **+10 Posture**
	- Regain **+1 additional Posture per round**
	- Unlock Arc Modules

Arc Wave: Release a wave of energy around you.
- **Type:** Combat Mantra  
- **Activation: Action**  
- **Ether:** 5  
- **Cooldown:** 5 rounds
	- 3d10 fire and 3d10 lightning damage in a 4.5 meter radius around you. 
	- If blocked, deals 4 posture damage.
	- 10% Penetration

Arc Beam: Shoot out a laser from your eyes that explodes after a second.
* **Type:** Combat Mantra  
* **Activation:** **Action**  
* **Ether:** 6  
* **Cooldown:** 4 rounds
	*  4d8 fire and 4d8 lightning damage to an enemy within 9 meters.
	* If blocked, deals 3 posture damage.
	* 15% Penetration

1. Arc Module: Dash: 
	- When Arc Suit is active, your dodges become Jet dodges, doubling the range dashed from 3 -> 6 meters. 
	- After a successful Dodge, you may make one melee attack as part of the same Reaction.
2. Arc Module: Eject:
	- When you would become staggered with Arc Suit active, you instead drop to 1 posture, and eject from your arcsuit without becoming staggered.
3. Arc Module: Guard: 
	- Blocking With your Arc Suit active negates all backstabs and allows you to block attacks from behind. 
	- You also take 25% less posture damage.
4. Arc Module: Leap: 
	- You can use high jump and long jump while standing still without triggering reactions. Your jump distance is also doubled.
