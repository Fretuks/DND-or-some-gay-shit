It is a rather varied [[Oaths|Oath]], given that its purpose is to extend the capabilities of what would be considered a Pathfinder.

> "You vow to never be bound to any oath; to live your life free of restraint. If free will is an illusion, why not make it a convincing one?"

The Oathless swear a paradox: a vow to never be bound by vows. Though they claim freedom from creed, power, and doctrine, the truth is more unsettling, **Oathlessness itself is an oath**, a carefully constructed illusion of autonomy. Those who walk this path reject the chains of higher powers not by breaking them, but by pretending they were never offered.

* This Oath grants the player two extra Wildcard Mantra slots, 10 Health and 20% Oath Mantra AC.
