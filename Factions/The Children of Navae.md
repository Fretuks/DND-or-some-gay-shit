The Children of Navae are a nomadic people who live in cold, remote regions far from most settlements. They are quiet, spiritual, and deeply connected to Frostdraw. The tribe worships Navae, a goddess associated with ice and shadow, whom they believe protects those who endure hardship in silence. Most Children of Navae are [[Gremor]]s, though outsiders are not unheard of. Their culture revolves around rituals, oral tradition, and strict respect for their beliefs.

Much of their early history is unclear, passed down through stories rather than written records. What _is_ known is that they created **The Way of Navae**, a unique martial art that blends frost, shadow, and unarmed combat into fluid, deliberate movements. The tribe has always wandered, following old routes through frozen lands. Recently, however, a group has settled on Meteor Isle. This decision has caused tension within the tribe, as many see permanent settlement as a rejection of their traditional way of life.

The Children of Navae avoid politics whenever possible, but the world has not left them alone. [[The Ministry]], [[The Knives of Eylis]], and [[The Central Authority]] all treat them as enemies. The Children themselves do not understand this hostility, viewing their attackers as ignorant outsiders who refuse to respect Navae’s path. On Meteor Isle, they have begun cooperating with [[The Hive]], hoping the jungle and its people will offer protection against forces that would see them wiped out.
